Meet the Team

We are a group of friends who are passionate about cars, engineering, and motorsports. Together, we are working to build, prepare, and race an endurance car in the 25 Hours of Thunderhill.

Why our team matters

We are not a company pretending to be bigger than we are. We are a small team of students and friends taking on a difficult engineering challenge together. Every sponsor, donated part, professional service, or connection helps us move closer to race day.

Students and Community Working Together Our goal is to represent the people and businesses who help us with professionalism, gratitude, and consistent documentation throughout the build.

Founder / Project Lead

Max

Age 16 · Redwood City, California

Max is from San Bruno and has lived in the Bay Area his whole life. He started 49th Parallel Racing by bringing the team together, organizing the project, and turning the idea of building an endurance race car into a real plan.

As project lead, Max is handling sponsor outreach, overall logistics, team coordination, website development, and long-term planning. He will also be responsible for purchasing and storing the car as the project moves from fundraising into the build phase.

Logistics / Planning

Xavier

Age 18 · Born in Oakland · University of British Columbia

Xavier was born in Oakland and now attends the University of British Columbia in Vancouver. Xavier and Max have been friends since birth, and this project is a culmination of shared dreams over their 16-year and counting friendship.

Xavier is the main logistics powerhouse on the team. He is helping with planning, budgeting, organization, and the practical decisions that need to happen before the car can be bought, built, tested, and raced.

Driver / Team Support

Thomas

Age 18 · Born in Denver · University of British Columbia

Thomas was born in Denver and now attends the University of British Columbia in Vancouver with Xavier. He loves cars and brings that same enthusiasm to the team as we work toward building a real endurance race car.

Thomas is planned to be one of the team’s drivers for the 25 Hours of Thunderhill. He will also help support the project through planning, preparation, and documentation as the build progresses.

Mechanical / Build Lead

Adam

Age 16 · Redwood City, California

Adam is from Redwood City and has lived in the Bay Area his whole life. Like the rest of the team, he loves cars and is excited to help turn the project from an idea into a working race car.

Adam will be a major part of the mechanical side of the project. He will help with the build, repairs, problem solving, and race preparation as the team works to make the car safe, reliable, and ready for endurance racing.
