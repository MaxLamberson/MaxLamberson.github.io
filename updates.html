Build Updates

Follow the project from planning and sponsor outreach to the car build, testing, and race weekend.

Update 01
Planning Phase

Sponsor Proposal Completed

What happened: We finished the first version of our sponsor proposal and began preparing outreach materials for local businesses, auto shops, and individual supporters.

What we learned: Sponsors need to understand not only what we are doing, but why the project matters and how their support helps.

Next step: Build our outreach list, finalize the website, and begin contacting potential sponsors.

Upcoming
Car Search

Vehicle Search Begins

What happens next: After initial fundraising, we will search for a suitable race car platform and inspect candidates for reliability, safety, cost, and parts availability.

Priority: Safety, reliability, budget control, and long-distance endurance potential.
